Creating an account

Home Page | Getting Started | Tutorials | Contact information
  1. First, head to wwww.squarespace.com. From there, select "Get Started" in the upper right hand corner of the screen.




  2. Once selected, you will be brought to their sets of templates. You can scroll through their displayed templates or can narrow your options by the type of website being created and by topic. In this section, you also have the option preview templates. When you have made a final decision, you can select "Start With" the specific template.





  3. If you do not have an account, you will be brought onto a sign up page. This page will give you the option to continue with Google, Apple, or Email. Underneath, you can find more options.



  4. Once you have created your account, you will be welcomed to your site and be prompted to edit the Site Title.
