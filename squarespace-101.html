PURPOSE

Digital portfolios are an effective way to demonstrate and provide evidence of work from prior experiences, both academic and professional. As stated by Paul Basken from The Chronicle of Higher Education,"knowledge production is supported through ePortfolios, which make normally unseen components of the learning process visible and give students agency, which increases motivation".

They also provide more context for potential clients or future employers, and can be included alongside a resume and other documentation. Content management systems, such as Squarespace, Wix, and WordPress, have made it much easier to display these works and have become increasingly popular.

A recent report revealed that WordPress powers 34% of the entire web.
Another report found in 2019, Wix had 120 million users .

For these reasons, I will be teaching how to use Squarespace to display your personal or work portfolios.
