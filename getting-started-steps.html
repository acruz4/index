CREATING AN ACCOUNT



Step One

First, head to Squarespace. From there, select "Get Started" in the upper right hand corner of the screen.

Step Two

Scroll through their displayed templates and select your favorite template. When finished, select "Start With".

Step Three

Continue with Google, Apple, Email, or select from the other options below.

Step Four

Once you have created your account, you will be welcomed to your site.
